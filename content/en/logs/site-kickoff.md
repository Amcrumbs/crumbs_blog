Today the personal site direction became Personal OS.

It is not just an article list, and it is not a marketing-style personal homepage. It should feel like a quiet workbench: modules on the left, content and tools in the center, recent entries and shortcuts nearby.

The first version keeps enough extension points:

- Notes and logs use file-based content.
- Private journals use password unlock.
- Tools show full UI and status.
- Guestbook messages support anonymous submission and moderation state.
